---
title: DFG supports the Emmy Noether project "The New Climate Divide"
date: 2024-11-10T07:14:00-04:00
summary: DFG funding confirmed for The New Climate Divide, including a six-year postdoc and two four-year PhD positions.
---

The Emmy Noether project **The New Climate Divide** has been awarded support by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG). The project includes a six-year postdoctoral researcher position and two four-year PhD positions that will be advertised soon.
